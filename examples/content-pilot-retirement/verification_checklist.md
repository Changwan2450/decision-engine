# Retirement Pilot Verification Checklist

Source script: `examples/content-pilot-retirement/script.md`

Purpose: turn every `[VERIFY]` and `[SOURCE NEEDED]` marker into a manual source-check queue before recording-ready rewrite.

Rules:

- Do not resolve markers without source review.
- Do not invent exact statistics.
- Prefer current Korean official or primary sources for pension, welfare, labor, housing, and household finance claims.
- If a claim cannot be verified, rewrite it as an illustrative scene or remove the factual implication.

## Summary

- `[VERIFY]` items: 6
- `[SOURCE NEEDED]` items: 6
- High-risk items: 6
- Primary risk areas: pension/welfare facts, labor/reemployment conditions, housing/household finance burden, broad claims about "many" or "적지 않은 사람들"

## VERIFY Items

### V1. Layered retirement pressure

- Marker: `[VERIFY]`
- Surrounding claim: "가족의 상황, 집의 상황, 건강의 상황, 일자리의 상황, 그리고 연금과 복지 제도가 실제로 언제부터 얼마나 도움이 되는지의 문제가 한꺼번에 겹칩니다."
- What needs verification: Whether current Korean retirement pressure can be responsibly framed as overlapping family, housing, health, labor, pension, and welfare factors.
- Suggested source type: official statistics; labor market report; housing/finance report; pension/welfare official source
- Risk level: high
- Suggested rewrite if not verified: "은퇴를 앞두고는 가족, 집, 건강, 일자리, 연금 같은 여러 질문을 함께 확인해야 합니다."

### V2. Retirement delayed by choice or identity

## SOURCE NEEDED Items

### S1. Housing cost as retirement interruption risk

- Marker: `[SOURCE NEEDED]`
- Surrounding claim: "집이 있어도 대출이 남아 있을 수 있고, 집이 없어도 월세나 전세 비용이 부담일 수 있습니다. 주거비가 높은 가정에서는 월급이 끊긴다는 것이 단순한 변화가 아니라 생활 전체의 위험처럼 느껴질 수 있습니다."
- What needs verification: Current Korean household debt, mortgage, rent, jeonse, or housing-cost burden context for middle-aged/older households.
- Suggested source type: housing/finance report; official statistics; credible news/report
- Risk level: high
- Suggested rewrite if not verified: "주거비나 대출이 남아 있는 집에서는 월급이 끊기는 일이 더 무겁게 느껴질 수 있습니다."

## Verification Priority

1. Pension/welfare eligibility and variability: V3, V4, V5, S3
2. Housing and household finance burden: S1
3. Labor participation and reemployment conditions: S2, S4
4. Family support and caregiving burden: S5, S6
5. Softer illustrative context: V1, V2, V6

## Recording-Ready Rewrite Rule

Before recording, each marker should become one of:

- a sourced, cautious factual sentence
- a softer illustrative sentence without scale claims
- a removed claim

Avoid retaining raw `[VERIFY]` or `[SOURCE NEEDED]` markers in the recording-ready narration.

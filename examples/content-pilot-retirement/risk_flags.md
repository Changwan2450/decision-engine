# Risk Flags

Pilot topic: `50대가 은퇴를 못 하는 진짜 이유`

## Political Overclaim Risk

Risk:

- The topic can become a claim about a single administration, party, or policy failure.

Mitigation:

- Do not assign sole political blame unless supported by verified evidence.
- Keep policy discussion specific and sourced.

## Financial Advice Risk

Risk:

- Viewers may interpret retirement, pension, debt, or work suggestions as personal financial advice.

Mitigation:

- Use general educational framing.
- Tell viewers to verify their own pension, benefits, debt, and work situation.
- Do not recommend specific investments, products, loans, or retirement timing.

## Pension / Welfare Factual Update Risk

Risk:

- Pension, welfare, and eligibility details can change.

Mitigation:

- Verify current official sources before final script.
- Avoid exact amounts, eligibility thresholds, or timelines until checked.

## Generation Conflict Tone Risk

Risk:

- The topic can turn into blaming adult children, parents, employers, or younger generations.

Mitigation:

- Present overlapping pressures.
- Avoid “because of children” or “because of older people” as a single-cause explanation.

## Fearmongering Risk

Risk:

- Titles and hooks can make viewers feel panic instead of recognition.

Mitigation:

- Avoid unsupported claims like “most people cannot retire.”
- Use calm language and practical next steps.

## Source Verification Risk

Risk:

- This draft packet does not include verified citations.

Mitigation:

- Run native research or import a report before final script.
- Mark imported reports as unverified until source checks are complete.
- Verify labor, pension, welfare, housing, and household finance claims.
